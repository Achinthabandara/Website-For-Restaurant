About Us

At The Golden Plate, we bring you the best of Sri Lankan cuisine, combining rich flavors and culinary traditions. Located at the heart of Colombo, we are been serving our customers with love and passion since 2023. We believe that every meal should be an unforgettable experience. We serve a diverse menu crafted from the freshest ingredients, blending traditional recipes with a modern twist. Whether your'e here for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a special occasion, we aim to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where culinary excellence and hospitality meet.

Our Story

The Golden Plate was born out of a passion for Sri Lankan culture and cuisine. Our goal is to provide a dining experience that not only satisfies your taste buds but also introduces you to the vibrant culinary history of Sri Lanka. From the aromatic spices to the freshly sourced ingredients, every dish tells a story.

Our Signature Dishes

Lamprais

A delightful blend of rice, curry, and a variety of accompaniments, wrapped in a banana leaf and baked to perfection.

Kottu Roti

A Sri Lankan street food classic, chopped roti with vegetables, egg, and your choice of meat, stir-fried with spices.

Pol Sambol

A spicy coconut relish that pairs perfectly with many Sri Lankan dishes, adding a burst of flavor to every bite.

Our Special Dish

Golden Hopper Delight

Experience the rich taste of Sri Lanka with our signature dish.

A gourmet take on the classic Sri Lankan hopper, the Golden Hopper Delight is a crispy, bowl-shaped hopper infused with turmeric (for its golden hue) and filled with a decadent coconut curry seafood medley—prawns, crab, and fish—all sautéed in rich Sri Lankan spices. Topped with a soft-poached egg and a drizzle of coconut cream, this dish is both visually stunning and packed with layers of flavor. Served with sides of Lunu Miris (spicy onion and chili sambol) and a mild Pol Sambol (coconut relish)
